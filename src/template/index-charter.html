SimpleSail Catamaran Yacht Charter

It is a great idea to spend an unforgettable holiday on a yacht under the control of captain-instructor of SimpleSail. Every day you will follow a route specially prepared for you. Every night you will go to a new location: a quiet fishing village or a city with pulsing nightlife. Or you can spend your nights on an ancor near the wild beaches.
